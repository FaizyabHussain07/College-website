Choose Your Field OF Study

Pre-Engineering

The Pre-Engineering program prepares students for engineering careers through core studies in mathematics, physics, and chemistry. Students are introduced to fields like Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Aeronautical, Architectural, and Chemical Engineering, with each area providing specialized insights and skills. Telecom and Space Engineering are also included, reflecting the diversity of options in modern engineering. Combining theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience, this program builds a strong foundation for future studies or careers in engineering.

Pre-Medical

The Pre-Medical program offers students a comprehensive foundation for careers in health sciences. Courses in biology, chemistry, and physics are complemented by specialized topics in areas such as physiotherapy, surgery, pharmacy, microbiology, and biochemistry. The curriculum also explores biotechnology and bioinformatics, bridging data and biology for medical research. With a mix of academics and practical labs, this program prepares students for medical school or allied health fields.

Computer Science

The Computer Science program builds essential scientific and analytical skills through courses in Physics, Computer Science, and Mathematics. Students learn programming languages, algorithms, and data structures, along with computer hardware and networking fundamentals. Physics and Mathematics support their technical knowledge, covering concepts like electricity and calculus. Through hands-on labs and projects, students gain a practical understanding of computing, preparing them for further studies or careers in the tech industry.

Commerce

The Commerce program provides foundational knowledge and practical skills in business and finance. The curriculum includes Accounting, Banking, and the Principles of Commerce, giving students insights into financial analysis, the workings of financial institutions, and market dynamics. Courses in Commercial Geography, Business Studies, Economics, and Statistics round out the program, offering a well-rounded education with a focus on real-world applications. The faculty emphasizes interactive learning through case studies, projects, and industry exposure, preparing students for various fields in commerce.
